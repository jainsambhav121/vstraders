# **App Name**: VSTRADERS

## Core Features:

- Product Management: Add, edit, or delete products (including details like name, description, price, images, and stock). Manage product categories and subcategories. Set product variations (e.g., size, color). Bulk upload/import products via CSV or other formats.
- Order Management: View and manage customer orders (e.g., pending, processing, shipped, delivered, or canceled). Update order status and send notifications to customers. Process refunds or returns. Generate invoices and shipping labels.
- User Management: Manage customer accounts (view, edit, or suspend accounts). Handle user roles and permissions (e.g., admin, editor, support staff). View customer purchase history and preferences.
- Inventory Management: Track stock levels and receive low-stock alerts. Update inventory automatically or manually. Manage suppliers and restocking.
- Payment and Transaction Management: Configure payment gateways (e.g., PayPal, Stripe, local payment methods). View transaction history and payment statuses. Handle refunds and disputes.
- Content Management: Manage website content (e.g., banners, homepage sliders, blogs). Edit static pages (e.g., About Us, Terms & Conditions). Add promotions, discounts, or coupon codes.
- Analytics and Reporting: Generate sales reports, monthly revenue reports, product statistics, user behavior, and conversion rate. Monitor top-performing products and categories. Gain insights for business decisions.
- Settings and Configuration: Configure website settings (e.g., site name, logo, theme colors, email notifications). Manage SEO settings, currency, tax rates, shipping options, and languages.
- Shipping Management: Manage shipping methods and rates (e.g., free shipping, flat rate, zone-based shipping). Integrate with delivery partners. Track shipping status and estimated delivery dates.
- Security and Moderation: Monitor and block suspicious activity (e.g., fraudulent orders). Manage authentication settings. Ensure data security and user privacy.
- Admin Dashboard: Provide a centralized dashboard to view sales data, recent orders, user activity, and quick shortcuts to important sections.
- Product Browsing and Search: Browse products by categories, subcategories, or filters (e.g., price, brand, ratings). Search bar with autocomplete and keyword suggestions. View product details (images, descriptions, reviews, and specifications).
- Shopping Cart: Add, remove, or update items in the cart. View cart summary (items, quantities, total price). Save cart for later or share wishlists.
- Checkout Process: Guest checkout or registered user checkout. Enter shipping and billing information. Select payment methods (e.g., credit/debit card, mobile payments, COD). Apply coupon codes or discounts. View order summary before confirming.
- User Account Management: Register or log in to an account. View and edit profile details (name, address, contact info). Track order history and status. Save multiple shipping addresses.
- Product Reviews and Ratings: Submit reviews and ratings for purchased products. View other customers’ reviews and ratings.
- Wishlist: Add products to a wishlist for future purchases. Share wishlist via email or social media.
- Notifications and Communication: Receive order confirmation, shipping updates, or promotional emails. Live chat or support ticket system for customer service.
- Responsive Design: Mobile-friendly interface for seamless browsing on phones and tablets. Fast loading times and intuitive navigation.
- Promotions and Recommendations: Display personalized product recommendations (e.g., “You might also like”). Show flash sales, discounts, or limited-time offers. Highlight trending or featured products.
- Localization: Support for multiple languages and currencies (e.g., Bangla, USD, BDT). Display region-specific pricing or shipping options.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep teal (#468499) to convey a sense of calm and reliability.
- Background color: Light teal (#E0F4F7), subtly desaturated, to complement the primary color without overwhelming the design.
- Accent color: Soft yellow (#F0E68C), analogous to the primary hue, providing gentle contrast.
- Font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, used for both headlines and body text.
- Clean, minimalist icons to represent product categories and actions.
- Grid-based layout for product listings, ensuring a clean and organized appearance.
- Subtle transitions and animations for user interactions, enhancing the shopping experience.
